Yogyakarta, Java, Indonesia


Yogyakarta is found on the island of Java, the most populous of the over 15,000 islands that make up Indonesia. It is special to me because this is where my father retired and lived for the last 10 years, where I lived and studied gamelan in 2015-2016, and where my little brother still resides.
